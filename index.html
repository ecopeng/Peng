My Research

The habitat environments can fundamentally affect where animals can go, thus can shape a range of ecological and evolutionary processes in populations, such as the patterns of social interactions among individuals as well as the spreads of pathogens and behaviors, etc.. My doctoral research is focusing on exploring such consequences of the physical habitat environments for social animals using theoretical approach.
